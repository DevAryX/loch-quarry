Visitors can take part in a wide range of land-based activities on and off site.

Hillwalking

Explore the breathtaking Scottish countryside with our guided hillwalking adventures. Suitable for all skill levels, our experienced instructors will take you through scenic trails, offering stunning views and a chance to connect with nature.

Max group size: 12

Ages 6+

Archery

Test your focus and precision with our archery sessions. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced archer, our instructors will guide you through the basics and help you hit the bullseye!

Max group size: 6

Ages 6+

Orienteering

Put your navigation skills to the test with our orienteering courses. Using a map and compass, you'll explore the terrain, solve challenges, and find hidden checkpoints. Perfect for teamwork and problem-solving!

Group size: 2+ (one map per group)

Ages 6+

Axe Throwing

Experience the thrill of axe throwing! Under the guidance of our instructors, you'll learn the techniques to safely throw axes and hit your target. A fun and unique activity for all ages.

Max group size: 6

Ages 10+
